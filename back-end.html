Overview

The Back-End track focuses on helping you produce PHP web applications using the Laravel framework. The goal of this 10 day track is to give you the last layer of experience before diving into the deep end of META+LAB client products.

Mentors: Luis Guzman, Matt Fritz, Nerces Kazandjian, Dr. Steven Fitzgerald


Day 1 Checklist
  1. META+LAB introductions
  2. Discuss the Explorer and Creator Roles and Expectations with Yazmin Peebles
  3. Create a LinkedIn account and/or connect with the META+LAB Organization and all Team Members
  4. Create a GitHub account and/or accept the META+LAB Organization invitation
  5. Accept invitation to join the META+LAB Canvas class and update all profile settings, including uploading your photo and recording your name pronunciation onto NameCoach
  6. Define your personal goals in META+LAB & email the team at: team@metalab.csun.edu
  7. Finalize schedules and availabilities.
